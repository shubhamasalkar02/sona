TATA COMPANY

Tata Motors Limited is India's largest automobile company, founded in 1945 by J.R.D. Tata and Ratan Tata. Headquartered in Mumbai, India, the company has grown into a $37 billion organization with subsidiaries including Jaguar Land Rover, Tata Daewoo and Tata Hispano. Tata Motors produces passenger cars, trucks, buses, defense vehicles and electric vehicles.The company's history began with its founding as Tata Engineering and Locomotive Company (TELCO) in 1945. In 1954, Tata collaborated with Mercedes-Benz, and in 1991, launched India's first indigenous car, the Tata Indica. The acquisition of Jaguar Land Rover in 2008 marked a significant milestone. The Tata Nano, launched in 2010, became the world's cheapest car.Tata Motors' brand portfolio includes Tata for passenger cars, Jaguar for luxury vehicles and Land Rover for SUVs and off-road vehicles. Commercial vehicle brands include Tata Daewoo in South Korea and Tata Hispano in Spain. Popular passenger car models comprise the Tata Indica, Indigo, Nano, Tiago, Tigor, Altroz, Punch and Safari. Commercial vehicles include the Tata Ace, 407, 709, Prima and Ultra, while electric vehicle offerings encompass the Tiago EV, Tigor EV and Nexon EV.As India's largest automobile company and the world's 26th largest automobile manufacturer, Tata Motors sells over 9 million vehicles annually. With exports to 170 countries, the company has received numerous awards for innovation, design and sustainability. Sustainability initiatives focus on electric vehicle development, hybrid and alternative fuel research, energy-efficient manufacturing and recycling. Financially, Tata Motors reported revenue of $37.4 billion and net income of $1.4 billion in 2022. Its market capitalization exceeds $20 billion. With global operations, the company has over 7,500 dealerships and service centers. Research and development centers in India and the UK focus on autonomous vehicles, connectivity and sustainability, bolstered by collaborations with global tech companies.

SCODA COMPANY

Škoda Auto, founded in 1895 by Václav Laurin and Václav Klement, is a renowned Czech automobile manufacturer. Headquartered in Mladá Boleslav, Czech Republic, it's a subsidiary of the Volkswagen Group since 1994. Škoda produces passenger cars, SUVs and electric vehicles, boasting a diverse portfolio.The company's history began with bicycle manufacturing in 1895, evolving into car production in 1905. Merging with Škoda Works in 1925 and joining Volkswagen in 1994 significantly expanded its global presence. Introducing the VisionD concept in 2011 marked a shift towards innovative designs.Škoda's model lineup includes Fabia, Rapid, Octavia, Karoq, Kodiaq and Enyaq electric SUV. Achievements include selling over 1.2 million vehicles annually, presence in 100+ markets and employing 15,000+. Award-winning designs, such as Red Dot and iF Design awards, underscore its commitment to innovation.Sustainability initiatives focus on electric vehicles, hybrid models, eco-friendly manufacturing and supply chain management. Financially, Škoda reported $17.4 billion revenue and $1.1 billion net income in 2022, with a market capitalization exceeding $10 billion.Globally, Škoda operates in the Czech Republic, China, India and Russia, exporting to Europe, Asia and Africa. Its 3,000+ dealerships and research centers in the Czech Republic and Germany drive innovation. Collaborations with Volkswagen and universities prioritize autonomous driving, connectivity and sustainability.Škoda's accolades include "Best Car Manufacturer" (2019, 2020) from What Car? Awards and "Design of the Year" (2020) from Red Dot Design Award.
